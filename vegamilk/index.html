QUALITY SOURCING

Our delivery process begins on dairy farms where cows are expertly milked under rigorous hygiene standards. After quality checks, the products are transported to our stores that maintain the freshness with temperature control.

FLEXIBLE ORDERING

We offer flexible ordering options to meet customer preferences, by ordering on-demand to prevent wastage, place advance orders, or opt for monthly subscription plans, ensuring convenience and affordability.

ON-TIME DELIVERY

We strategically position local stores for efficient distribution and quick deliveries. Our dedicated delivery team ensures punctual deliveries, upholding our commitment to providing fresh dairy on time, every time.

How Does it Work?

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your android device. (currently not available on App Store)
  2. Search for Vegamilk on Google Play Store and Download it.
  3. Open the downloaded app and Login/Signup with your account.
  4. Order from our nearby stores and partners.
  5. Wait for our delivery guy to bring you a burst of freshness.
